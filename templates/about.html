Interested in trapping a cat?

Do you have a cat that you are interested in trying to catch and fix? We can provide some help with that task. Depending on where you live, there might be free services that will provide you with a trap and cover the cost of the surgery. If you live in Hamilton County, or the surrounding area Ohio Alley Cat offers a great service. They provide free spay and neutering and vaccines. You many need to investigate a similar service in your area.

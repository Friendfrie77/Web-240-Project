Looking to Adopt a Cat?

Are you looking to adopt a cat? While the majority of our cats are not suitably for adoption, we do have a small selection of cats that are suitable for adoption. The cats on this list are our friendliest cats that get along with others well. All cats that we adopt out come with full medical information and are up to date on all their shots. The process for adopting with us includes an in-person screening, as we want to make sure the cats are going to a good home, you will also get to spend time with the cat or cats you are looking at in a closed off area to see how well you get along. Interested in trapping a cat?

Do you have a cat that you are interested in trying to catch and fix? We can provide some help with that task. Depending on where you live, there might be free services that will provide you with a trap and cover the cost of the surgery. If you live in Hamilton County, or the surrounding area Ohio Alley Cat offers a great service. They provide free spay and neutering and vaccines. You many need to investigate a similar service in your area.

We have traps for rent!

Thanks to donations we are now offering a trap rental service. The cost to rent a trap is $5, but when renting we as for a payment of $55 to cover the price of the trap if you fall to return it. When you pick up the trap, we will show you how to set it, and good ways to bait the trap. They are perfect for taking the cat to the vet to get a checkup, or to get fixed. Please contact us if you would like to rent a trap.

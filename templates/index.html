Our Mission

We believe that every cat should have a chance to at a good life. Wither that is giving a feral cat a new home with a loving family, or a great life within our sanctuary. On top of giving these cats a great home, we wanted to offer the opportunity to give the public a chance to feel the joy that comes with helping change an animal’s life for the better. We also strive to educate the public on how to properly deal with feral cats via trapping and fixing said cats.

Total cats spayed or neutered this year: 

Spaying and neutering cats is one of the best ways to help control the population of cats and other animals. It is a human way of making sure that they do not reproduce. This not only helps to reduce the number of feral cats around, but also helps to protect other wildlife from the cats. We offer a free service for spaying and neutering cats. You can find out more here.

While the vast majority of our cats are not suitably for adoption, we do get some that would just love to find a loving family. For a small fee, to help cover some of our operating cost you can bring home your own furry friend. You can find a little about the cats that we have open for adoption here.

The Cats

All one hundred of our cats have been either trapped by volunteers or give to us by the public or other shelters. All our cats are fixed and are up to date on all their shots. We provide a safe space for our cats to live where they have all the food and water they need. We try our best to get the cats suitable for adoption good homes. You can find more information about what we do for our cats, and see the current cats up for adoption here.
